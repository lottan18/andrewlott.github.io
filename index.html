• CV

• PhD student at the University of Georgia. (Go dawgs!)

• My advisors are Ákos Magyar and Giorgis Petridis.

• My research is in the intersection of number theory, harmonic analysis, and combinatorics. Specifically, I am interested in applications of harmonic analysis and ergodic theory to problems in density Ramsey theory. I especially care about problems concerning prime numbers.
